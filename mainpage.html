About Us

Happy Tails promotes responsible pet ownership, the health and safety of people and animals, and bringing families together. We provide temporary shelter for "lost" owned or stray animals and opportunities for adoption of these animals. Additionally, we house and provide medical care for impounded dogs and cats.


We strive to save the lives of all healthy, treatable, and manageable dogs and cats in our community. We work very closely with our nonprofit rescue adoption partners, other local animal shelters, and our volunteer foster caretakers.

Our Mission

At Happy Tails, we are dedicated to rescuing abandoned and abused animals, providing them with medical care, rehabilitation, and finding them loving forever homes. We believe in promoting responsible pet ownership and educating the community on the importance of spaying and neutering their pets. Our mission is to give every animal a second chance at a happy and healthy life.
